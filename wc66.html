World Cup 1966

The Route to the Final





In July 1966 the eighth staging of the FIFA World Cup was held in England. Sir Alf Ramsey was manager and the Beatles were at number one with Paperback Writer. Sir Alf took a team of 22 players to the finals and this is their journey.
